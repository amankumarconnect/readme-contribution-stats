# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as contributors and maintainers of **Readme Contribution Stats**, pledge to create a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone who participates in this project.

We value collaboration, learning, and constructive feedback, and we are committed to making open-source participation a positive experience for all.

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of positive behavior include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Providing constructive and thoughtful feedback
- Gracefully accepting criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project

### Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Any behavior that is unprofessional or disruptive in a collaborative setting

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior
- Reviewing issues, pull requests, and discussions fairly
- Taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and repositories
- All interactions related to this project, whether online or offline

---

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainer.

Reports will be reviewed and handled respectfully and confidentially.  
Actions may include warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from the project, depending on the severity of the violation.
